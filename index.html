Use this guide to help understand the differences between cold and flu symptoms. Remember that symptoms can vary between individuals and these are general guidelines only.

Symptom Cold Flu
Stuffy or Runny Nose Yes Sometimes
Sneezing Yes Sometimes
Cough Yes, with green or yellow mucus Yes, a dry cough
Sore Throat Yes Sometimes
Body Aches Mild head & body aches Severe aches all over
Nausea No Sometimes
Fever Rare for adults Yes
Chills & Sweats No Yes
When do symptoms come on? Slowly, over a few days Fast, within hours

Important Medical Information

  • This chart is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice
  • Symptoms can vary significantly between individuals
  • Some symptoms may overlap between cold, flu, and other illnesses including COVID-19
  • Consult a healthcare provider if symptoms are severe, persist, or worsen
  • Seek immediate medical attention for difficulty breathing, chest pain, or other serious symptoms
  • Consider getting tested for COVID-19 if you have respiratory symptoms
  • Vaccination is the best protection against influenza
